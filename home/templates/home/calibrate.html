Sensor Calibration Instructions for AQI Monitoring:
  1. Ensure the sensor is placed in a well-ventilated area to avoid calibration errors. Avoid placing it in areas with high humidity or direct sunlight.
  2. Expose the sensor to a known reference gas (for PM2.5, CO, CO2, Ozone (O3) to ensure the readings are within expected ranges.
  3. Verify sensor response by checking the systems output against known AQI values for the current air quality conditions.
  4. Run a final verification test with the sensor exposed to varying air quality conditions (clean, moderate, and polluted air) to ensure it is responsive across the full range of expected AQI levels.
